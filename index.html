Learn About Donation

Before you donate blood, make sure to:

  • Be in good health and feeling well.
  • Weigh at least 110 pounds (50 kg).
  • Not have smoked cigarettes or consumed alcohol within 24 hours prior to donating.
  • Wait at least 56 days after your last donation.

Learn About Requests

If you need to request blood, please ensure:

  • You provide all necessary information, including your blood type.
  • You have a valid reason for requesting blood.
  • You know where to go for your blood transfusion.
